A little about us...
The Tybee Sean Shanty is a seaside restaurant serving American-style cuisine, including burgers, brisket, salads, and the freshest fish dishes on the island. Seafood is caught locally daily. Join us for a fun, casual atmosphere while enjoying the sun on your back or a break from the beach in our air conditioned dining room. We are a family-friendly restaurant, and we even invite you to bring Fido to join you when dining on the deck.
