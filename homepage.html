A little about us...
The Tybee Sean Shanty is a seaside restaurant serving American-style cuisine, including burgers, brisket, salads, and the freshest fish dishes on the island. Seafood is caught locally daily. Join us for a fun, casual atmosphere in the sun, or in the air conditioning. We offer both indoor and outdoor seating so you can choose depending on your wants or needs.
